How do I apply for the NFL Extra Points Credit Card?





If you’re ready to apply, choose your team card, then select the Apply Now button.







How do I pick an NFL team card?





If you have one favorite NFL team, it’s easy! Just scroll through the card options, pick your team and apply. Your team-branded card should arrive in a couple weeks, then you can start spending and earning. If you’re a fan of more than one team, sorry but you'll have to choose a favorite (don’t worry, we won’t tell the other team).







Does this credit card have an annual fee?





No, there is no annual fee⁵ associated with the NFL Extra Points card.  







Will I earn rewards on my Special Financing Ticket purchases?





Yes! You will enjoy special financing on ticket purchases of $250 or more made at identified ticket providers.³ Plus, you'll earn points for every dollar on your ticket purchases made with your NFL Extra Points Card.







Is there a maximum amount of reward points I can earn?





While you can earn rewards points every time you use your card, some offers have maximum limits as part of the promotion. Please reference the Rewards Terms and Conditions for full program details.







Do reward points expire?





Your NFL rewards points will expire if not redeemed after 5 years. Rewards are subject to Rewards Terms and Conditions.







If I’ve already received my NFL Extra Points card, how can I activate?





Find your Account Center to activate online immediately or dial the number on your card. When you activate, please have your new card and information ready. If you have authorized buyers, you can also activate their cards, you’ll just need the last four digits of each card.







How can I pay my NFL Extra Points credit card bill?





You can pay your bill online 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Find your Account Center or go to the link on the back of your card. To avoid a late fee, your payment must be successfully submitted by 5 p.m., Eastern Time (ET) on or before your due date.







I received my card, but I’d like to change the team. How do I do that?





Once you’ve registered for Account Center access, you can request a different team card on the Account page. Find your Account Center or look for the link on the back of your card.
